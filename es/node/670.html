WeXchange pitch competition goes into the second round

WeXchange pitch competition goes into the second round

Noviembre 19, 2014
By
Georg Neumann

 

En español

As it was last year, the pitch competition, organized with our partner NXTPLabs, will be the highlight of WeXchange, the premier forum for kickstarting women entrepreneurs who work in Latin America and the Caribbean.

We’ve now reached the first milestone: From more than 70 applicants from 15 countries, we’ve selected a list of 12 semifinalists.

Here’s the list of these 12 dynamic women entrepreneurs and their startups:

1. Wendy Ruiz Cofiño, Digital Awareness, Guatemala

2. Carolina Medina Gutiérrez,  SokoText, Colombia

3. Catalina Estrada Penagos, Ciencuadras.com,  Colombia

4. Priscilla Maciel,  Almashopping, Argentina

5. Sandra Muriel Bourgeois Gutierrez de la Torre, MiCuento, Chile

6. Lorena Amarante,  OM Latam, Argentina

7. Cristina Palacios,  Aventones, México

8. María Leal,  Zynn, Guatemala

9. Anna Heim, Monolibre, USA

10. Maia Chacra,  Gorsh, Argentina

11. Cecilia Retegui, Zolvers, Argentina

12. Susana Cipriota, Funnely, Argentina

Who will make it to the final round? We’ll let you know in early December who will pitch their businesses to investors and our international jury of experts in Miami. 

The competition is for startups that focus on technology and innovation, have been operating for less than 3 years, and have received less than US$ 1 million in funding. Learn more about the pitch competition here.

Como el año pasado, el pitch competition, organizado por nuestro socio NXTPLabs, será el evento destacado en WeXchange, el principal foro para impulsar a las mujeres emprendedoras que trabajan en América Latina y el Caribe.
